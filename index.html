Our Story

We met in the most unexpected way. What started as a class lesson for Naa's mum brought us into the same space, Naa quietly in her corner and me focused on the task at hand. At first, it did not seem like anything special.


But over time, a friendship grew. And somewhere along the journey, life had other plans. What once felt ordinary became something truly meaningful, something we now know was the beginning of forever.


And the rest is a beautiful story we are still writing.

God wrote a better story than we ever imagined.
